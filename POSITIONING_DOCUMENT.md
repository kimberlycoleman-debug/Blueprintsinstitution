## IV. THE ECONOMIC MODEL

### Free for individuals. Funded by institutions and grants.

**For the individual believer:** $0. No tuition. No fees. Pure access.

**Funded by three streams:**

🌿 **Foundation grants** — Nonprofit and faith-based foundations funding spiritual formation work with measurable outcomes.

🌿 **Major donors and kingdom stewards** — Individual believers and ministry partners investing in formation as kingdom infrastructure.

🌿 **Institutional partners** — Churches, ministries, and businesses paying a per-student fee to enroll their leaders. **Institutional revenue funds free individual seats.**

### The Dual-Track Model

> **Institutions pay so individuals don't have to.**

When a church enrolls 10 of their leaders through institutional partnership, they pay an institutional fee. That fee:

1. Covers their leaders' formation costs
2. Generates surplus that funds **free seats for individual believers** who can't pay
3. Sustains the Institute so it remains free at the point of entry for every believer

**The more institutions enroll, the more free individual seats open.**

This solves four problems no Christian formation program has solved at scale:
- **Access** — formation should not be a class privilege
- **Sustainability** — institutional revenue funds ongoing operations
- **Rigor** — paying institutions demand measurable outcomes, which serves individuals too
- **Scale** — the model compounds as institutional adoption grows

## V. THE PROGRAM STRUCTURE

**12 months · 4 quarters · 16 modules · 80 lessons**

### Quarter 1: ALPHA — Identity
*Discover who you are in Christ.* Creates the Identity Blueprint Statement™.

### Quarter 2: FORMATION — Maturity
*Develop spiritual disciplines, emotional health, and authentic community.*

### Quarter 3: MATURITY — Healing
*Experience inner healing, freedom from cycles, and holistic wholeness.*

### Quarter 4: MINISTRY — Activation
*Step into calling. Launch ministry. Be commissioned.* Creates the Purpose Statement™ and Ministry Launch Plan™.

### Three Capstone Deliverables

Every graduate produces:
1. **Identity Blueprint Statement™** (end of Q1) — written declaration of who God designed them to be
2. **Purpose Statement™** (end of Q4) — written declaration of calling and assignment
3. **Ministry Launch Plan™** (end of Q4) — concrete action steps for kingdom contribution

### Certificate of Completion

Graduates receive a **Certificate of Completion in Discipleship Training** at the Commissioning Ceremony — public declaration of calling, prophetic words spoken, commissioning prayer, and formal sending.

## VI. THE FORMATION TRACKING SYSTEM

**This is the founding distinctive made operational.**

Every student is tracked across:

🌿 **80 lessons** — completion status, depth engagement, time spent

🌿 **Reflection patterns** — written entries, AI Formation Companion responses, themes that emerge over 12 months

🌿 **Depth progression** — every objective scored on the **B.L.U.E.P.R.I.N.T.™ Depth Scale**:
- **Foundation** (introduced)
- **Discernment** (understood)
- **Practice** (lived)
- **Embodiment** (integrated)

🌿 **Dual scoring system** — numeric (0-100) plus qualitative (not_demonstrated, emerging, demonstrated, embodied)

🌿 **Capstone deliverables** — Identity Blueprint, Purpose Statement, Ministry Plan reviewed by facilitator and AI

🌿 **Attendance and engagement** — weekly session attendance, cohort participation, peer support

🌿 **Facilitator narrative assessments** — pastoral observations, breakthrough moments, areas needing care

### What this produces

For the first time, spiritual formation generates **structured data**. That data fuels:

- **Grant reports** with measurable outcomes
- **Institutional partnership accountability** — sponsoring organizations see real transformation
- **Personal student progress** — believers can see their own formation arc
- **Founder intelligence** — cross-cohort pattern detection at the institutional level
- **Curriculum improvement** — what works, what doesn't, lesson-by-lesson
- **The kingdom's first formation dataset** — what spiritual transformation actually looks like at scale
