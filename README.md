# The Blueprint Discipleship Institute

> "For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do." — Ephesians 2:10

A production-grade discipleship operating system that empowers believers to discover their God-given identity, develop Christ-like character, experience holistic healing, and activate their unique calling.

## What This Is

The Blueprint Discipleship Institute is a 12-month intensive discipleship training platform. Not a curriculum app. Not a learning management system. A **formation operating system** — institutional grade, AI-powered, built for transformation that can be measured and proven.

## The Mission

**Matthew 28:19-20** — Go therefore and make disciples of all nations.

This platform makes that mandate executable at scale.

## Architecture

**Stack:**
- Next.js 15 App Router (TypeScript)
- Supabase (Postgres + Auth + Storage)
- OpenAI API (GPT-4) for AI-assisted formation
- Tailwind CSS (Blueprints brand: warm, hopeful, human)

**Three Roles, Three Experiences:**

1. **Student** — Going through the 12-month formation pathway
2. **Facilitator** — Leading a cohort, reviewing reflections, holding space
3. **Admin** — Overseeing multiple cohorts, institutional reporting

## Program Structure

**12 Months · 4 Quarters · 16 Modules · 80 Lessons**

- **Q1: IDENTITY** — Who you are in Christ
- **Q2: FORMATION** — Spiritual maturity and character
- **Q3: HEALING** — Inner healing and wholeness
- **Q4: ACTIVATION** — Stepping into calling and assignment

## Capstone Deliverables

Every student produces:
- **Identity Blueprint Statement** (Q1)
- **Purpose Statement** (Q4)
- **Ministry Launch Plan** (Q4)

Every student receives:
- **Certificate of Completion in Discipleship Training**
- **Commissioning Ceremony**

## AI Capabilities

AI is woven throughout — not as a gimmick, but as a formation companion:

- **Identity Blueprint Builder** — Synthesizes student reflections into clarifying language
- **Reflection Companion** — Responds to journal entries with deeper prompts
- **Purpose Statement Generator** — Weaves together quarterly work
- **Ministry Plan Assistant** — Turns calling into actionable steps
- **Facilitator Insights** — Summarizes cohort reflection patterns
- **Admin Intelligence** — Surfaces formation health across cohorts
